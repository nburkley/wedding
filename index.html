A little about the bride and groom.

After years of living in China, I decided to do some traveling through Central and South America. After bumping into a handsome Irish guy at the top of a volcano in Nicaragua I eneded up taking a detour through Ireland. Fast-forward a few years and he's putting a ring on my finger in a hot-air baloon over the Catalonian countryside.

I had planned a three month backpacking trip through Central America. A year later I was still traveling and getting ready for a trip to Austraila to meet Jo's family. A few months, later we're starting a new life in Berlin, a city I'd never even visited. Life never turns out like you'd expect, and I wouldn't change it for one second.

A weekend with friends

In a little village in Portugal

“The world is a book and those who do not travel read only one page.”

After spending a quite a few years in various countries and continents, our friends are now dotted all over the world. We want to try and bring everyone together in one place and celebrate our marriage over a weekend. It may well be the only time in our lives that both our families meet. We want to make sure that everyone get's to know each other, and we think we've found the perfect place to do so...

We discovered the village of Aldeia da Pedralva through some Portuguese friends of ours. It's a small, restored village in the middle of the countryside and we've got it all to ourselves for the whole weekend. We can get married in the town square and dance in the streets!

The Weekend Plan

This is a rough plan for the weekend. Apart from the dates (and the people getting married), nothing is set in stone - so details may vary slightly. It should still give you an idea of what to expect.

Friday 15th - Welcome to Aldeia da Pedralva

After a long journey, unpack your bags, relax and join us for some food and drinks. Say hello to old friends and meet new ones. Come and meet everyone in the village where food and drinks will be provided.

Saturday 16th - Let's have a wedding

This is why we're in Portugal. On Saturday afternoon it's time for the main event. Come gather in the town square, and celebrate our marriage with us. The wedding ceremony will be followed by drinks, dinner and a party into the early hours of the morning.

Sunday 17th - Wind down BBQ

Let's wind things down and spend one last evening together. We're putting on a BBQ to reflect on and recover from the weekend. It'll be a chance to spend one last evening together before we all head off on our separate ways.

Beach time?

Don't want to leave the party just yet? We'll be heading for some time by the beach for the remainder of the week. Come join us, the Australian crew and anyone else that would like to come along for a few days of fun in sun in the Algarve. We plan on renting out some villas and soaking up some sun by the beach in Alvor. Drop us a line if you're interested in joining.

The Village

Set in the country countryside of the Algarve, the small village of Pedralva almost in ruins a few years ago. Since 2006, the village has been completely restored with over thirty of the original cottages available.
